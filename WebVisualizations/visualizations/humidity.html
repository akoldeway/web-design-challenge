Latitude vs. Humidity

When reviewing the Latitude vs. Humidity chart we find that humidity does not have a strong correlation to latitude. Despite no definitive correlation, we do see cities in the northern hemisphere have a higher concentration of cities with humidities at or above 80% than those in the southern hemisphere.
